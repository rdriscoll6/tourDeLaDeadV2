Day 9: Newport

June 7th, 2024

0 miles

I got up at 7:20 and started to clean up and pack my things. My friend got up a little later. Since it was his last day, we decided not to ride and instead enjoy Newport. We biked back up the huge bridge from yesterday. I was annoyed that we had to go backward, but looking back, my friend was pretty overwhelmed since he had a lot of decisions to make that day.

We got breakfast and then went to a park where we hung out for the majority of the day. During that time, I went grocery shopping and took money out of the ATM. My friend got us a motel for the night. Once we got the bikes in the room, I talked to my mother about a possible game plan going forward, and she said family back home supported me as well.
