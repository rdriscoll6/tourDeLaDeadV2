Day 17: Burlington to Leggett

June 15th, 2024

48 Miles

Left the park at 9:10 AM, finishing the Avenue of the Giants with lots of hills—climbing up and coasting down. My legs felt strong today, not even tired from the ride. Climbing is frustrating though; my heavy panniers slow me down a lot.

Reached Garberville after 20 miles, found chain lube at a toy store, and had lunch at a café. Southern California was noticeable—less green and hotter, just like Michalina said.

Arrived at the park for the night with no cell service, but the ranger station had free Wi-Fi. Caught up with mom and friends. Explored a cool creek in the park. Tomorrow's supposed to be the biggest climb of the trip, but I'm feeling ready. Ate dinner early and hit the sack for tomorrow's challenge.
