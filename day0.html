Day 0: Seattle

May 29th, 2024

My mother dropped me off at the Rochester airport. There, I met up with my friend, and we were able to check in our bikes and boxes filled with panniers and camping gear. We got through TSA without any issues and boarded our connecting flight to Newark. There was no turning back now.
